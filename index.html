🧨 How to Play

The goal of the game is to uncover all safe cells without clicking on a mine and put a flag on all cells with mine.
If you click a mine, you lose a life. Lose all lives – and the game is over.
Each number represents the amount of mines in the surrounding cells
The first click is always safe – no mines will be placed there.

🛠️ Special Features

# Feature Description
1 😀Restart button Resets the game, timer, lives, hints, flags and everything else.
2 🛟Lives You start with 3 lives. Each mine click reduces a life. The game ends only when all lives are lost.
3 🚩Flagging Right-click or long-press (2 seconds on mobile) to place/remove a flag on suspicious cells.
4 💡Hints 3 regular hints. Activate one and click a cell to temporarily reveal it and its neighbors.
5 🔲Mega Hint One-time powerful hint. Select two cells(left to right only!) to define an area that will be temporarily revealed.
6 Removes 3 random mines from the board and updates the numbers accordingly (one-time use). works only at medium and expert level.
7 🏝️Safe Click Up to 3 uses per game. Highlights a guaranteed safe cell temporarily.
8 ↩️Undo Go back one step (up to 3 times), even after revealing a mine.
9 🕓Timer Starts on your first click. Tracks how long you take to complete the game.
